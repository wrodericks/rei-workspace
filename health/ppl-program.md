# 12-Week PPL Program — Jun 6 to Sep 8, 2026

**Goal:** +2kg skeletal muscle (46.1 → 48kg) while maintaining fat loss deficit
**Baseline:** 115.3kg | 37.8% BF | 46.1kg skeletal muscle | 40.0% muscle %

## Nutrition (every week, no exceptions)
- **Calories:** 1,900–2,000 kcal (up to 2,100 on heavy training days)
- **Protein:** 140–150g daily — revised Jun 30 (fat loss primary goal, muscle preservation)
- **Creatine:** 5g daily, every day
- **Kitchen closes at 8pm**

## Progressive Overload Rules
- Log every session: exercise, sets, reps, weight
- Each week: add weight OR add 1 rep per set to at least one exercise
- If you can do all sets at the top of the rep range, add weight next session
- Never miss a log entry — if you don't track it, it doesn't count

## Phase 1 — Toronto (Weeks 1–4, Jun 6 – Jul 2)
**Schedule:** 3x/week first 2 weeks → 6x/week weeks 3–4
