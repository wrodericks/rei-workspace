## Variables / Notes
- **Veggies change batch to batch** — celery, carrots, mushrooms are flexible
- **Acid:** lemon juice preferred over apple cider vinegar (same macros, negligible difference)
- **Always low-sodium:** low-sodium beans (10mg/125ml) + low-sodium tomatoes (5mg/125ml) + low-sodium chili powder. No exceptions.
