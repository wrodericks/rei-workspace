# Bodyweight Program — Japan (Indoor)

**Use when:** no gym access, hotel room, transit days
**Goal:** maintain muscle, keep metabolism active
**Duration:** 30–40 min
**Equipment:** none (chair/bed for some exercises)

---

## The Circuit (3 rounds)

### Push (Chest / Shoulders / Triceps)
| Exercise | Reps | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Push-ups | 15–20 | Standard — chest to floor, full extension |
| Pike Push-ups | 10–12 | Hips high, head toward floor — shoulders |
| Diamond Push-ups | 10–12 | Hands close together — triceps |

### Pull (Back / Biceps)
| Exercise | Reps | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Inverted Rows (under desk/table) | 10–15 | Lie under sturdy table, pull chest to edge |
| Superman Hold | 10 × 3 sec | Lie face down, lift chest + legs — lower back/rear delts |
| Towel Bicep Curl (isometric) | 10 each arm | Step on towel, curl against resistance |

### Legs
| Exercise | Reps | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bodyweight Squat | 20–25 | Full depth, controlled |
| Bulgarian Split Squat | 12 each leg | Rear foot on bed/chair — quads + glutes |
| Lunges | 12 each leg | Walking or stationary — great cardio, quads + glutes |
| Glute Bridge | 20 | Lie on back, drive hips up — hamstrings/glutes |
| Calf Raises | 25–30 | On edge of step or flat floor |

### Core
| Exercise | Reps/Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Plank | 45–60 sec | Elbows or hands |
| Dead Bug | 10 each side | Opposite arm/leg, slow and controlled |
| Reverse Crunch | 15–20 | Hips up, not crunching neck |

---

## How to Use
- **3 rounds** of the full circuit with 60 sec rest between exercises
- Can split into push/pull/legs on separate days if preferred
- Add reps or reduce rest time as it gets easier
- Do before showering — 35 min max

## Progressive Overload (bodyweight style)
- More reps → slower tempo → pause at top/bottom → single-leg/arm variations
- When push-ups get easy: elevate feet, add a pause, or do archer push-ups
