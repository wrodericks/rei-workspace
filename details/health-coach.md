# AI Health Coach — Commercial Product

Seed idea: 2026-04-13. Full spec: health-app/PRODUCT_SPEC.md

## Founding Philosophy
Build something that works for Warren first. Learning and personal effectiveness are primary goals. Money follows value — don't optimize prematurely.

## Positioning
Conversational AI health coach for 50+ professionals. Weight loss + energy maximization. Active planning partner, not passive tracker.

## Go-to-Market
B2C first (personal network beta), enterprise-ready architecture from day one but not built yet.

## Tech Stack
Expo (React Native) + Supabase + OpenAI (GPT-4o + Vision + Whisper) + Expo Push Notifications + Railway

## Development Approach
Solo (Warren) + Claude Code via Rei. No co-founder in V1.

## Core V1 Features
- Nutrition plan (AI-suggested, user-overridable)
- Multimodal food logging (photo, voice, barcode, favourites)
- Daily dashboard with calorie deficit
- Wearable integration (Fitbit/Apple Watch/Garmin/smart scales)
- Proactive AI coaching, sleep tracking, water tracking, reporting/trends

## Key Differentiators
- Frictionless logging removes the #1 reason people quit
- Japanese food database
- Energy goal alongside weight loss
- Personalization via ML over time
- Built for the physiology and psychology of people over 50, not 25-year-olds chasing abs
- Potential brand angle: longevity, Japanese health culture

## Out of Scope V1
Medical advice, workout logging, inactivity nudges, formal calendar integration, voice UI, Japanese brand development, ML personalization
